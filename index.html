STRENGTHS & WEAKNESS

Know about me

Organization

Organization is key. I thrive on structure, prioritizing tasks, and managing time efficiently. This skillset helps me achieve goals and excel in any role.

Collaborative

Throughout my life, I've worked in teams, from student projects to my current work environment. This experience has helped me learn how to communicate effectively, delegate tasks, and learn from others.

Self-critical

My self-critical nature drives me to excel, but it can also be a weakness, leading to excessive pressure and potential for self-doubt.

Overload

I often overload myself, juggling work and personal commitments. It's a challenge to stay focused and avoid burnout, but I'm learning to prioritize and delegate.

You only fail when you stop trying

Work areas of interest

Automation

As a computer engineer, automation is my secret weapon. I love creating programs that work for me on repetitive tasks, freeing up human potential for more complex challenges.

Data Analysis

I love turning data into insights. By exploring datasets, I uncover patterns and trends that drive business decisions. Data analysis is the key to unlocking hidden value.

Business Intelligence

I create solutions that turn data into actionable knowledge. Business intelligence helps organizations make informed decisions, improve performance, and gain a competitive edge.

MDM

Master data management is the foundation for data-driven success. I ensure data accuracy, consistency, and accessibility. By creating a single source of truth, I improve data quality and reliability.
