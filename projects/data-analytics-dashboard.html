Power BI commercial analytics dashboard

End-to-end solution integrating POS sales, CRM, and quarterly targets to provide daily tracking of key indicators.

Dashboard highlights

  • Tabular model with more than 120 DAX measures for margins, churn, and forecasts.
  • Advanced segmentations by channel, region, and product family.
  • Proactive alerts published through Power BI Service and Teams.

Dataset and processes

The data pipeline was built with Azure Data Factory and Synapse notebooks to clean transactional information, enrich with master data, and publish the certified dataset.
